- **UNLOAD overshoot — ROUTE 1 branch-A discharge SHIPPED (2026-07-01).** The branch-A fit-inductor L_a (~23 µH PLL) carries the full DC load current, so a hard UNLOAD leaves it sourcing the OLD current into Cout → the rail overshoots. A pure OUTPUT-side clamp CANNOT win (overshoot-depth × recovery-time ~ const, since dI_L/dt = V_across_L/L_a): local-Spectre baseline — no clamp → **+302 mV, ABOVE the 0.98 V supply**, ~2 µs; an output high-side clamp → bounded to +24 mV but **HANGS 4.7 µs**. Route 1 DRAINS the inductor instead: a **source-gated, voltage-BOUNDED reverse EMF** added in series to the RESISTOR-form branch-A regulation, `V(regnode,vrg) <+ R_a·I + gate(vov)·srcblk(I)·ovVmax·tanh((ovR/ovVmax)·I)` (gate=one-sided deadzone in the overshoot vov=V(o,vrg); srcblk=`0.5(1−tanh(I/ovIsc))` ~1 sourcing/dump, ~0 sinking/abuse). It applies a reverse EMF to the whole series inductor chain (main L_a + ladder) → the +302 mV above-supply kick is **CAPPED to +34 mV (≤ supply, monotone settle, no 4 µs hang); VCO +124 mV → +28.5 mV**. ZERO value+slope at the OP (vov<0 for every load) → **AC/PSRR 0.0000 %, clean load-dip 0.0000 µV** (Spectre-verified, both loads); the EMF clamp preserves the 1/R_a DC pin so a **+40 mA floating-rail abuse-sink stays at 0.824 V (no runaway)** — the srcblk + the bound are what keep it off the reverted-Icomp de-pin failure. **KEY (adversarial-review) refinement: `ovVdz` is a LARGE-SIGNAL TRANSPARENCY FLOOR, not a DC-droop margin.** The dump also engages on the positive half of a large-signal PERIODIC ripple, so ovVdz MUST sit above the validated ripple regime or it clips the spur/PSRR/Zout SPECTRA the model produces (Spectre: ovVdz=3 mV → 16 mV clip-distortion on a 20 mV-above ripple; ovVdz=**25 mV** (default, 2.5× the ~10 mV char) → 0.002 mV, transparent). The cost: ovVdz is also where the unload overshoot SETTLES → sub-µs-to-5mV (which needs ovVdz<5 mV) was TRADED for the spectral transparency of the model's primary function — the fix still kills the two real pathologies (above-supply kick + 4 µs hang), just with a tens-of-mV monotone ~2 µs settle.
